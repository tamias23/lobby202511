# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use dedalthegame.com. We are based in Brussels, Belgium, and comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 1. Data We Collect
We only collect the minimum data necessary to provide a fair and functional gaming experience:

*   **Account Information:** If you register, we store your username, email address, and an encrypted password hash.
*   **Profile Settings:** We store your preferences (e.g., timezone, theme, board settings) to provide a consistent experience across sessions.
*   **Gameplay Data:** We store match history, ratings, and tournament participation to maintain leaderboards and game integrity.
*   **Technical Data (Anti-Abuse):** We temporarily store your **IP address** to enforce fair usage limits on bot games and prevent service abuse.

## 2. Why We Collect It (Legal Basis)
*   **Contractual Necessity:** To create your account, manage your games, and track your ratings.
*   **Legitimate Interest:** To protect our infrastructure from abuse (e.g., IP-based rate limiting) and ensure a fair environment for all players.

## 3. Data Retention
*   **IP Addresses:** Technical logs used for rate-limiting (e.g., the 24-hour bot game limit) are stored in a temporary cache (Valkey) and automatically expire/delete after 24 hours.
*   **Account Data:** Stored as long as your account is active. You can request account deletion at any time.

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR)
As a user in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:
*   **Access** the data we hold about you.
*   **Rectify** (correct) inaccurate data.
*   **Erase** your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
*   **Object** to or restrict certain processing.
*   **Data Portability** (request a copy of your data in a structured format).

To exercise these rights, please contact the Data Controller (see below).

## 5. Security
We use industry-standard encryption and secure database practices (PostgreSQL and Valkey) to protect your data from unauthorized access.
